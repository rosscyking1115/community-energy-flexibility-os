# Roadmap and freeze

## Completed

- **v0.1.0 — portfolio demonstrator:** domain model, rule-based and LP/MILP optimisation, FastAPI, Next.js and Streamlit interfaces, reports, dbt/Snowflake/Dagster paths, and Power BI example.
- **v0.2.0 — credibility closeout:** structured source provenance, explicit live/fallback presentation, robustness terminology, conditional ex-post semantics, report-export tests, claim ledger, evidence pack, and unified Community Energy Flex naming.

## Feature freeze

The project is now feature frozen. Only correctness, security/dependency maintenance, deployment reliability, and evidence-preserving documentation corrections are in scope.

Research ideas are recorded in [FUTURE_RESEARCH.md](FUTURE_RESEARCH.md), not presented as committed product work.
